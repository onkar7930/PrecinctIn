# PrecinctIn - Control Your Feed

PrecinctIn allows you to curate your LinkedIn feed by hiding posts that contain specific keywords. Whether you want to avoid spoilers, specific topics, or just reduce noise, PrecinctIn gives you the control to mask content you don't want to see.

--------------------------------------------------
## FEATURES:
--------------------------------------------------

● **Keyword Filtering:** Easily add keywords (comma-separated) to block specific topics.
● **Instant Masking:** Posts containing your keywords are instantly covered with a polite overlay.
● **Reveal on Demand:** Curious? Click "Reveal" to peek at the hidden content.
● **Simple Toggle:** Turn the extension on or off with a single click.

--------------------------------------------------
## WHAT'S NEW IN VERSION 1.2:
--------------------------------------------------

● **"Reveal" Functionality Split:** The original "Reveal" button is now two distinct buttons: "Peek" and "Reveal"!
● **Peek Button:** Want a quick look? The "Peek" button (light blue) temporarily shows a hidden post for 5 seconds before it's hidden again.
● **Reveal Button:** Ready to unmask a post for good? The "Reveal" button (darker blue) will permanently show the post.
